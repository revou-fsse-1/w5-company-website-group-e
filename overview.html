About RevoU Chapters


Embracing our mission of helping you accelerate your career in the new digital economy, we are aware that getting connected with 5000+ networks of professionals in the tech industry which RevoU has now, is a must and a privilege you need.

We create **RevoU Chapters**, a sub-community divided geographically, with a dream to bring together you with other alumni who share a common interest in the tech industry and provide further opportunities for you. Each RevoU Chapter will be led by alumni volunteers consisting of one Chapter Leader and its team members.


Why we're creating RevoU Chapters?â€¦


  1. Providing a networking platform for you and fellow RevoU alumni who live in the same geographical region to easily meet offline to open up more collaborations and opportunities.
  2. Providing additional value and supports for you, be it in career progression through our series of chapter activities.
  3. Engaging and reaching a broader network of alumni to understand better how we could improve our Community to further supports you throughout your career

So, that is why our chapter's activity will be focused on three areas: Socializing, Network Expanding, & Contributing

  1. Socializing: RevoU Chapters is expected to be a community which the member can create social activities for alumni meet together, share ideas and new insights which will benefits Chapter's member or the society in general
  2. Network Expanding: RevoU Chapters will continously give best effort in expanding the network of our alumni, so they can connect with fellow tech workforce and open many new opportunities to support each other
  3. Contributing: RevoU Chapters is a project from and for alumni, therefore it'll keep building a sustainable ecosystem for alumni to contribute and involve directly in building RevoU Community, so they will feel welcomed and proud as a RevoU alumni.
